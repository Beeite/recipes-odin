Kabsa

Decription

a nice and relatively quick saudi dish made with roasted chicken and well seasoned red rice,
perfect for people that dont have much time nor experince but still want a full on meal

Ingredients

  • Chicken, Preferablly whole thats around 1000g
  • Basmati rice
  • Tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Waraq ghar
  • Lumi
  • Mixed spices
  • Salt and black pepper!

ps: You need a pressure cooker and a stove


Prepration

Get your chicken out and let it sit inside a bowl of water with 5l and lemon for at least 30 mins


Get your rice and let and keep washing it until the water you wash it with is clear

Then let it rest in slightly warm water for 10 mins minimum

Steps

First step is to put the onions and simmer them for a few mins then add tomatoes
then add the chicken and let it simmer for a few more mins
then add your spices both powder and otherwise (keep a lumi to the side)
add enough water to submerge the entire chicken then close the lid and let it cook for 10 mins
open the lid and add your rice to the mix, you can make sure the water is only 1-3 centimeters above the rice, you can add/remove water to do that
and cook for 10 mins
